About us

About Foster Research

Foster Research is dedicated to provide a global level platform for the Scholars, Students, Researchers and Business Entities to make them experience a qualitative collective knowledge sharing and networking through conducting International Conferences, Seminars, Workshops, Symposia and other related events.

  What we do?

The Foster Research is a young professional meetings organizer pioneered in the planning and execution of academic meetings for the betterment of scientific community and the world as a whole.

 Our Aim

The Foster Research aims to gather the key players in all sectors and to provide a unique forum for discussion of the latest developments, finding solutions, refreshing knowledge and providing plenty of networking opportunities, a chance to meet and interact with leading researchers as well as most influential minds in the world.

 Our Mission

With the help of dedicated and proficient professionals who have years of experience in organizing successful conferences across the globe, we manage our events successfully with participation of scholars and professionals from different fields.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I still be able to interact with the presenters during the sessions?

Yes, the speakers will be available to answer questions during the sessions that are presented over the conference dates

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. Your registration fee will include an "on demand" feature, so you can access any session you choose from the conference library after the scheduled conference dates.

What does the registration fee include?

The registration fee includes admission to all sessions of the conference and the cost of meals and refreshments.

Can I pay by check or cash?

Our online system only accepts payment via credit card, stripe and/or PayPal.

How to register for a conference organized by Foster Research?

You may register for the meeting through the online registration form. All conference participants must register by specific dates mentioned in the respective conference webpage or their presentation information will not appear in the conference program. For more information, please refer to the conference webpage or contact the Event Manager.
